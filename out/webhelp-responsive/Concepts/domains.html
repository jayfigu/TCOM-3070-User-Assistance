Domains

Now that you have your store setup, you will need a way to show this store to your customers. A domain will allow people to access your store through the internet.

A domain is the website address that customers can visit to browse and purchase from your store online. With Shopify you have a few options for domains depending on your preferences and starting point.

You can have a domain through Shopify. An example of the URL for this is examplestore.myshopify.com.

Alternatively, you can have a custom domain which you can either purchase a new URL or have one transferred to Shopify. An example of a URL for this is examplestore.com.
