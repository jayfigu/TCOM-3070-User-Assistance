      You can use Shopify's analytics and reports to learn about your sales and customers in great detail. The types of analytics and reports that you can view depend on your Shopify Plan. Remove an online sales channel

  1. Click Settings on the Shopify admin, and then click Sales channels.
  2. Find the name of the sales channel you want to remove.
  3. Click remove channel
